Introduction

Welcome to CampusNotes. This Privacy Policy is here to tell you how we protect the information you trust us with so you can comfortably manage your academic notes.

02

Information We Collect

We only collect data that helps make your academic life easier. This includes your account details (Email, User Identification) and metadata about the study resources you upload and download.

Account Login Upload History
03

How We Use Information

The primary reason we collect your information is to personalize your study experience. We use data to recommend high-quality notes and process your requests more efficiently.

Study Insights Notes Personalization
04

Data Sharing

CampusNotes has a strict policy: We do not sell your personal information. We only share anonymized analytical data with educational partners to improve our platform's academic reach.

Zero Selling Safety First
05

Cookies

We use small cookies solely for session management. They help our system remember that you're logged in as you move from one subject note to another.

06

Data Security

Every note and account on our platform is protected by SSL encryption. We perform regular security audits to ensure that your academic identity and content remain secure.

07

User Rights

You are the owner of your data. You have the total right to access, edit, or permanently remove your personal info and any uploaded materials from our database.

Right to Erasure Data Access
08

Children’s Privacy

CampusNotes is intended for university/college-level students. We do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. If discovered, such data is deleted immediately.

09

Changes to Policy

As we add new learning features, our privacy standards evolve. We will notify our users about significant updates via our dashboard or direct email notification.

Contact Us

Questions about your data or our security practices? Our dedicated privacy and support team is ready to respond to your specific inquiries.
