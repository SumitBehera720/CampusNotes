Help Center

Explore the features of CampusNotes and learn how to master your studies.

📚

Browse Notes

▼

What is it? A powerful repository of study materials curated by students like you.

How to use: Search by subject, college, or tags. Filter by difficulty (e.g., Exam-Oriented) to find exactly what you need to ace your next test.

📤

Upload & Share

▼

What is it? Your personal contribution to the community. Share your PDFs, hand-written scans, or PYQs.

How to use: Click "Upload" in the menu, drag and drop your file, add a catchy title, and you're good to go. Once approved, you'll start earning points!

⚖️

Note Comparison

▼

What is it? Our "Expert Pick" tool that helps you choose between two similar notes.

How to use: Enter two note IDs or link to see a side-by-side breakdown of ratings, download count, and quality metrics before you hit download.

🏆

Leaderboard

▼

What is it? The "Hall of Fame" for the top contributors of CampusNotes.

How to use: Earn points by uploading high-quality notes and getting downloads. The top 3 users get featured on the global leaderboard!

📋

Note Requests

▼

What is it? A community wish-list for notes that don't exist yet.

How to use: Can't find notes for a specific topic? Post a request! Other students can see your request and upload matching notes for you.

🎖️

Badges & Rewards

▼

What is it? A gamified reward system for your academic journey.

How to use: Unlock badges like "Early Bird" or "Expert Uploader" automatically as you use the site. These badge colors appear on your profile!
