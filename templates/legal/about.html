Hi, I’m Sumit.

I’m a developer passionate about building useful platforms for students. I created CampusNotes as a solo developer with one goal in mind: Simplicity.

CampusNotes is a platform where students can easily share and discover academic notes. I built this because I believe studying should be a collaborative journey, not a solo struggle.

This platform is designed to help you access quality study materials, learn faster, and support each other throughout your academic journey.
